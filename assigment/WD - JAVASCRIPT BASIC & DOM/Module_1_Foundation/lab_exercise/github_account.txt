Module 1 - Lab Exercise

Task: Create Github account & make repository.

Steps to complete this task:
1. Go to https://github.com/
2. Click on the "Sign up" button at the top right corner.
3. Enter your email address, create a strong password, and choose a unique username.
4. Verify your account by solving the puzzle and clicking the verification link sent to your email.
5. Once logged in, click the "+" icon at the top right and select "New repository".
6. Give your repository a name (e.g., "frontend-assignments").
7. Choose to make it Public or Private, and optionally check "Add a README file".
8. Click "Create repository".

Your GitHub account and first repository are now ready to store your project code!

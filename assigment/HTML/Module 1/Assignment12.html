SUZUKI HAYABUSA

The Suzuki Hayabusa is an iconic hyperbike known for its blistering speed and unmatched performance. Powered by a 1,340cc inline-four engine, it produces around 190 horsepower and 111 lb-ft of torque, delivering an exhilarating ride with smooth acceleration and remarkable top-end power.


BMW S1000rr

The BMW S1000RR is a high-performance sportbike designed for thrill-seekers and track enthusiasts. Equipped with a 999cc inline-four engine, it delivers impressive power and torque, generating around 205 horsepower and 83 lb-ft of torque.


DUCATI PANIGALE V4

The Ducati Panigale V4 is a flagship sportbike that combines cutting-edge technology, breathtaking performance, and Italian craftsmanship. Powered by a 1,103cc V4 engine derived from Ducati's MotoGP racing technology, it produces a staggering 214 horsepower and 91 lb-ft of torque, providing blistering acceleration and top-tier performance on both the track and the road.
